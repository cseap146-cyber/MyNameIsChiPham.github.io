Who I Am

Most people call me Chelsea. I am an undergraduate Business Economics student at York University, interested in how markets, incentives, and human behavior shape decision making. I enjoy analyzing problems, understanding how people and businesses respond to change, and finding practical, data-driven solutions.

Outside of academics, I work as a Barista at Starbucks. Working in a fast paced, high volume environment has taught me how to stay calm under pressure, multitask efficiently, and communicate clearly with both customers and my team. It also gives me a real-world perspective on concepts like pricing, demand, labor, and operations ideas that connect directly back to what I study in economics. Curious, hardworking, and eager to learn, I’m always looking for ways to grow, whether that’s building new skills, taking on challenges, or better understanding the world through an economic lens.
